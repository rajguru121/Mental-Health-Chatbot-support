Features

Built around your wellbeing

Every feature was designed with one goal — to make you feel heard, safe, and supported.

Empathetic Conversations

Context-aware responses that actually follow the thread of your emotions — not generic replies, but real empathy.

Real-time Risk Detection

An ML model trained on real data classifies each message as Low, Medium, or High risk — instantly, every time.

Guardian Alerts

When high-risk messages are detected, your trusted guardian gets an immediate email with crisis resources.

Coping Exercises

Breathing exercises, grounding techniques, and movement prompts delivered in-context based on how you're feeling.

Conversation History

Named chat sessions let you track your emotional journey over time and revisit past conversations.

Privacy First

Passwords are hashed, data stays in your own database, and your conversations are never shared or sold.
